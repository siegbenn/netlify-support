Tell about how you made your site and why you chose the tools you did. Briefly explain a challenge you experienced in setting up this site and how you solved it.

Tools

I used Jekyll to generate my site. I chose Jekyll because I didn't want my site to be a blog and it's the simplest of the bunch to configure. Because vanilla HTML is valid, it was really easy to scaffold the layout. For styling, I chose Tachyons. It's a simple style sheet that I've been working with lately. I think it has a very unintrusive style and is easy to use.

Challenge

For the redirect bonus question, I didn't know that the _redirects file needed to be inside of the build directory. Once I realized that, fixing it was easy.
