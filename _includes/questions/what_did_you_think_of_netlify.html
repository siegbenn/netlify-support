What did you think of our service during the time you used it?

The Good

  • The build process is fantastic. I've never had a build fail.
  • The performance is great, all of my sites are super fast.
  • It's simple. Netlify does static hosting and does it well.

The Okay

  • The documentation is pretty good. I think it could be more readable and / or searchable. Having 20+ root items in the side navigation of the documentation page is overwhelming.
  • I would like to see the uptime of the system. What are the SLA agreements for the different plan levels? I know it's easier to use than S3 and CloudFront but how does the performance and uptime compare?

The Bad

  • A few weeks ago, there was an outage that briefly removed all of my sites. Downtime should be actively communicated to customers.
